FPV Racing Drone

Skills

  • SolidWorks CAD
  • Rapid Prototyping
  • Design Optimization
  • Leadership
  • Troubleshooting

As part of the ASME IAM3D international competition, I led the design of a fully 3D-printed FPV racing drone capable of carrying and deploying a payload mid-flight. Acting as Drone Body Design Lead, I oversaw the CAD modeling, structural analysis, and hands-on prototyping of the vehicle's airframe—balancing strength, weight, and aerodynamic performance to meet competition constraints.

Using hand calculations and structural FEA, I validated frame geometry and material selection before printing each prototype. Rapid iteration through 3D printing and field testing allowed our team to fine-tune parameters such as thrust-to-weight ratio, battery efficiency, and payload stability. My work focused on lightweight optimization, modular assembly, and quick repairability under racing conditions.

In the final stages, our team faced a catastrophic motor failure the day before competition. I coordinated emergency sourcing and repair efforts—troubleshooting, soldering, and rebuilding through the night to deliver a flight-ready drone by sunrise. The drone successfully completed the course, earning 1st place in Design Report and 2nd overall among approximately 25 international teams.
